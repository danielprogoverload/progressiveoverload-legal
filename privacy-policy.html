Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Progressive Overload is operated by ProgressiveOverload LLC ("we", "our", "us", "the app"), a workout tracking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it's protected.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Password — encrypted by Supabase Auth, never stored in plain text, never accessible to us

Profile Information

During onboarding, you provide:

  • Name, age, gender
  • Body weight and height
  • Fitness experience level, training goal, available equipment
  • Preferred unit system (lbs/kg)

Workout Data

When you use the app, we collect:

  • Exercises performed, sets, reps, weights, RPE (intensity)
  • Workout dates, duration, and completion status
  • Personal records (PRs)
  • Training splits and programs
  • AI-generated workout summaries

Body Measurements (Optional)

If you choose to track body composition:

  • Body weight over time, body fat percentage
  • Circumference measurements (chest, waist, arms, thighs)

AI Chat History

Conversations with the Overload Assistant are saved to provide continuity between sessions. You can clear your chat history at any time.

AI Coach Notes

After each workout, the app generates a brief summary of your training patterns, preferences, and progress using AI. These "coach notes" are stored on your profile and used to personalize future AI coaching responses. Coach notes contain observations like strength trends, exercise preferences, and training consistency — they do not contain sensitive personal information beyond what you've already provided through your workout data. Pro subscribers can view a polished version of what the coach has learned in the "Your Coach Knows" section.

Split Import via URL (Optional)

When you paste a URL into the app's split creation flow, our server-side proxy fetches the publicly available content of that URL on your behalf. The URL and the fetched content are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for parsing into a structured training routine. Neither the URL nor the fetched content is persisted in our database or logs. Only the final training split (which you review and save) is stored on your account.

We do not fetch content from sites whose robots.txt disallows automated access, from private networks, or from URLs that fail our security validation. Social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook) are not supported for URL import due to their terms of service — the app directs users to screen-record and use Video Import instead. For abuse pattern detection, we log a non-reversible hash prefix of imported URLs (not the URL itself).

3. Third-Party Services

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

Your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database. Supabase provides authentication, data storage, and serverless functions. All data is protected by Row Level Security — users can only access their own data. Supabase Privacy Policy

Anthropic (AI Processing)

When you use AI features (workout summaries, the Overload Assistant, AI split generation, coach notes generation, proactive coaching insights, URL split import), your profile information and workout data are sent to Anthropic's API (Claude) for processing. This data is sent through a secure server-side proxy — your device never contacts Anthropic directly. Anthropic does not use API data to train their models. Anthropic Privacy Policy

Data sent to Anthropic includes: your name, age, gender, weight, height, experience level, training goal, recent workout history, coach notes, your message, and — when you import a split from a URL — the publicly available text content fetched from that URL. This data is used solely to generate personalized responses and is not stored by Anthropic beyond the request.

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

If you subscribe to Overload Pro, RevenueCat manages the subscription through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier (not your email or name) to link purchases to your account. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Apple (App Store & Payments)

Subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment information (credit card, Apple Pay details). Apple Privacy Policy

Expo (Development Platform)

The app is built with Expo (React Native). Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and performance data. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • API keys are stored server-side only, never in the app code
  • All AI requests are authenticated via JWT and routed through a secure server proxy
  • Row Level Security is enforced on every database table — users can only read, write, update, and delete their own data
  • Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth and never stored in plain text
  • No data is stored on your device — all data lives in the cloud database
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption is used for all data in transit
  • URL imports are fetched through a security-hardened server-side proxy: https-only, DNS resolution validated against private network ranges (SSRF protection), robots.txt respected, size and timeout limits enforced, redirect chains capped and re-validated at each hop, and requests identify themselves with an honest User-Agent so site owners can choose to allow or deny access

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed from our database, including your authentication record, profile, workout history, training splits, coach notes, and chat history. This deletion is irreversible.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all your data is visible within the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data — available in Settings within the app
  • Clear your AI chat history at any time
  • Modify your profile information at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Progressive Overload is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app includes age-based safety limits for exercise recommendations (users under 14 receive bodyweight-only recommendations).

8. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or camera
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not store any data on your device
  • We do not store URLs you import from or the content of those URLs

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
