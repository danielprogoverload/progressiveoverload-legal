Privacy Policy

Progressive Overload App

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Progressive Overload ("we", "our", "the app") is a workout tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (securely hashed). During onboarding, you may provide your name, age, gender, height, weight, experience level, fitness goal, and available equipment. This information is used to personalize your training experience.

Workout Data: We collect the workout data you log, including exercises performed, weights, sets, reps, intensity ratings (RPE), workout duration, and dates. This data is used to track your progress, provide AI-powered recommendations, and generate share cards.

Body Measurements: You may optionally log body measurements such as weight, body fat percentage, chest, waist, arm, and thigh measurements. This data is used solely to help you track your body composition over time.

AI Interactions: When you use the Overload Assistant (Pro feature), your conversation messages are stored to provide conversation continuity. Your workout data and profile information are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate personalized recommendations. Anthropic does not use your data to train their models.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the app's core functionality: tracking workouts, generating AI-powered progressive overload recommendations, creating training splits, and producing share cards. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not display advertisements in the app.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with industry-standard encryption. Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text. All data transmission between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at supabase.com/privacy.

Anthropic: We use Anthropic's Claude AI to power the Overload Assistant, workout recommendations, and split generation. Your profile and workout data is sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalized responses. Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.

Apple (In-App Purchases): If you subscribe to Overload Pro, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. When you delete your account, all of your data — including your profile, workout history, saved splits, and chat history — is permanently deleted from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Progressive Overload is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time through the app. You can update your profile information in the Profile screen and delete your account entirely from the same screen. If you need assistance with data requests, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
